Creator Sponsorship Guide

Learn how to pitch, prepare media kits, and secure brand deals successfully.

1. Prepare Your Media Kit

Before contacting any brand, create a clean one-page PDF summarizing your channel's metrics. Include:

  • Channel links & active platform statistics (average views, subscriber count).
  • Audience demographics (top countries, age groups).
  • Past examples of sponsored integrations or reviews (if any).
  • Direct contact email and professional socials.

2. Craft a Professional Pitch

Do not spam brands with generic templates.

  • Use the **Pitch Helper** on CoMatch to copy a pre-formatted structure.
  • Customize the brackets `[Name]`, `[Link]` with your real data.
  • Focus on *how your audience benefits the brand* rather than what you want to get.
  • Keep it brief: Marketing teams receive hundreds of emails daily.

3. Understand Deal Structures

Sponsorship types depend mostly on your audience size:

  • Product Gifting (Micro Creators): Perfect for setups and peripherals. Brands send free gear (mouses, headsets, chairs) in exchange for video reviews or stream overlay integration.
  • Affiliate (Commission): You get a custom discount code/link. You earn a percentage (usually 5% to 15%) per checkout. Excellent way to prove your conversions to the brand.
  • Flat Fee (Macro Creators): Contractual fixed payment per integration (e.g. 60-second video slot). Usually starts when you have an active average of 5,000+ views per video.

4. When and How to Follow Up

Patience is key.

  • Give the partnerships team **7 to 10 business days** to review your pitch.
  • If you don't receive a reply, send a polite follow-up email replying to your original thread, sharing a new stream milestone or a recent popular video link.
  • If they decline or don't reply after two attempts, thank them and focus on building your audience. You can pitch again in 3 to 6 months!

Platform DNA & FAQ

💰 Why is CoMatch completely free?

We believe B2B matchmaking should be open and direct, without gatekeepers, paywalls, or expensive agency middlemen. Our infrastructure costs are minimal, funded entirely through open-source donations, voluntary sponsorships, and developer contributions. We will never sell your data or charge subscription fees.

🔒 How is my startup or project data protected?

Your privacy is our priority. Unlike other platforms, when you type your startup details or traction metrics to generate pitches, the formatting is processed **entirely inside your local browser**. Your proprietary descriptions are never stored in our cloud databases. You retain 100% control over your data.

🤝 How can I contribute or support the project?

CoMatch is community-driven! You can help by submitting verified brands or active VC/angel investor guidelines using our AI Submission Form. If you are a developer or marketer, you can contribute code or resources directly to our open-source GitHub repository.
